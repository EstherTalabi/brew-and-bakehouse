OUR STORY

A small shop with strong opinions about coffee.

We started Brew & Bakehouse in a small corner space with one good espresso machine and one simple belief - that every morning deserves something worth waking up for.

Today we roast in small batches, source directly from local farms, and bake every pastry fresh, same day.
